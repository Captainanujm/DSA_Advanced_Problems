## 📖 About

This repository contains a **comprehensive collection of DSA problems** organized by:
- **Topic** (Arrays, Trees, Graphs, DP, etc.)
- **Pattern** (Two Pointers, Sliding Window, DFS/BFS, etc.)
- **Difficulty** (Easy, Medium, Hard)

Each problem is handpicked based on frequency in real SDE interviews at companies like Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta, Apple, and top startups.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Follow the study plan** at the bottom for structured preparation
2. **Focus on patterns**, not just individual problems
3. **Solve 3-5 problems per pattern** to master the technique
4. **Track your progress** using the checkboxes

```

- [x] Completed
```
